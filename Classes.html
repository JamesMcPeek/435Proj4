Warrior

Warriors of Middle-earth are known to possess great strength and honor. Your goal is to defend those around you, while eliminating all threats. As the shadows spread across the lands, so shall your name. You will be trained in the skills of melee combat by only the finest of warriors.

Wizard

Wizards are the holders of ancient wisdom and possess unmatched potential. You are capable of harvesting immense power from the world around you and will destroy evil by using powerful spells. This power is easily corruptable, thus your mentor will be one of the honorable remaining Maiar.

Burglar

Burglars are crafty individuals with the ability to unknowingly slip into the dark. Your strength stems from trickery, attacks from the shadows, and unmatched sleight of hand. You will be mentored by the most cunning of burglars we were able to find.
